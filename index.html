Denji

About Denji

Denji, a teenage devil hunter working for the Yakuza to pay his late father's debts, was betrayed and nearly killed. In his dying moments, Pochita made a pact, giving Denji the Chainsaw Devil's power.

Denji's characteristics

  1. He lacks wisdom but makes up for in his empathy and readiness.
  2. He dreams to have good food to eat, shelter over his head, clothes to wear and a girl to marry
  3. He is a kind-hearted person despite his shortcomings
